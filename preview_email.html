💺 About seat assignment
When buying on FIFA Collect you purchase a CATEGORY (CAT1, CAT2, etc.) that defines your stadium zone — not a specific seat. FIFA assigns your exact seat 24-48 hours before the match. CAT1 = best zones near the field. CAT2 = mid-tier. CAT3/4 = upper tiers.
